Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Degauss ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safe-guard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Minimization

Degauss is designed to be a private, offline-first experience. We do not require you to create an account to use the App.

2. Information We Collect

a. Usage Data (Local)

The App stores your focus sessions, generated artifacts, and statistics locally on your device using Apple's Core Data and UserDefaults technologies. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via iCloud backups.

b. Device Information

We do not collect personal device identifiers (IDFA) or track your location.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics, Mixpanel) or advertising networks.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect identification information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@degauss.app.
