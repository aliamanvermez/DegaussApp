COLLECTING AND USING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We generally do NOT ask You to provide specific personally identifiable information (such as Email or Phone Number) because the App functions offline and locally.

Usage Data

Usage Data is generated when using the Service.

Degauss stores your Focus Session history, Statistics (e.g., total focus time), and Generated Artifacts locally on your Device using Apple's Core Data and UserDefaults technologies.

We do not upload this data to a central server. It remains on your device unless you choose to back it up via iCloud backups or share specific items manually.

Information Collected while Using the Application

While using Our Application, in order to provide features of Our Application, We may request access to:

  • Photo Library: To verify or save generated Artifacts (images) to your device's gallery.
  • Notifications: To alert you when a focus timer completes.
  • Motion Activity: To detect when the phone is placed face-down (for the "De-gauss" mechanic).

You can enable or disable access to this information at any time, through Your Device settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data (if any is collected) for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including to monitor the usage of our Service.
  • To manage Your requests: To attend and manage Your requests to Us.

Retention of Your Personal Data

The Company will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

Since Degauss is an offline-first app, your data is primarily retained on your local device. If you delete the App, your local data (Artifacts, Stats) will be deleted unless backed up to iCloud.
