Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Select your desired duration using the dial, then place your phone face down on a flat surface. The timer will start automatically.

What happens if I pick up my phone?

If you lift your phone during a session, the magnetic field becomes "Unstable". You have a few seconds to place it back down, or the session will fail and your crystal will shatter.

Where is my data stored?

Degauss is designed with privacy first. All your session data, artifacts, and stats are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

How do I delete an artifact?

Go to the Gallery tab (the grid icon). Tap and hold on any artifact card to see the delete option.
